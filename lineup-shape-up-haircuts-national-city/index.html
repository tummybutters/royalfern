FAQ

Questions clients ask before an edge-up appointment

Is a lineup the same thing as a full haircut?

Not always. A lineup can be part of a full haircut, but it can also be a detail-focused refresh that sharpens the outline without fully changing the cut.

Can lineups look too harsh?

They can if the hairline gets pushed back too aggressively. Good edge work should look clean and deliberate while still working with your natural growth pattern.

Do lineups work for curly or textured hair?

Yes. In fact, textured hair often benefits a lot from careful line work as long as the outline is shaped with restraint and not forced into an unnatural pattern.
